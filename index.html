  • Competence Development: Training effectiveness and skill assessment

  • Cultural Factors: Cross-cultural audit practices

üìä Research Methodologies

  • Mixed Methods: Combined approaches for comprehensive insights
  • Longitudinal: Long-term studies tracking changes over time
  • Comparative: Cross-industry and cross-cultural studies
  • Action Research: Collaborative research with practitioners
